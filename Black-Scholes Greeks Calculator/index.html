Understanding the Greeks

The Greeks measure different dimensions of risk in an options position.

Δ

Delta

Sensitivity to price changes in the underlying asset.

Γ

Gamma

Rate of change of Delta. Measures convexity.

Θ

Theta

Time decay. How value erodes with time.

V

Vega

Sensitivity to implied volatility changes.

ρ

Rho

Sensitivity to interest rate changes.

Built with pure JavaScript. Based on the Black-Scholes-Merton model (1973).
