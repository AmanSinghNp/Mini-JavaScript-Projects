🧱 Breakout

Physics-based brick breaking game

🎮 Controls

A/D Move paddle
Arrow Keys Move paddle
Mouse Move paddle
P Pause/Resume
R Restart (when game over)

🎯 Objective

Break all the bricks without letting the ball fall off the screen!

  • Hit the ball with different parts of the paddle to control angle
  • Higher rows of bricks give more points
  • You have 3 lives - use them wisely!

Part of the Physics-engine demo collection
