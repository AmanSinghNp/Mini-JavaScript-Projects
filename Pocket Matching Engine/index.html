How Price-Time Priority Works

1

Price Priority

The best price always gets matched first. For buys, higher prices have priority. For sells, lower prices have priority.

2

Time Priority

At the same price level, earlier orders get matched first (FIFO - First In, First Out).

3

Partial Fills

Orders can be partially filled. The remaining quantity stays in the book until fully matched or cancelled.
