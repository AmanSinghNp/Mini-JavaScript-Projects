Ingredients

  • check
    2 lbs Roma tomatoes, halved
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 quart vegetable broth

Instructions

1

Preheat and Prep

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Arrange the halved tomatoes and peeled garlic cloves on the baking sheet.

2

Roast the Vegetables

Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper. Roast for 40-45 minutes, until the tomatoes are soft and slightly charred.

3

Sauté Aromatics

While the tomatoes are roasting, heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.

4

Simmer and Blend

Add the roasted tomatoes, garlic, and any juices from the pan into the pot. Pour in the vegetable broth, bring to a simmer, and cook for 10 minutes. Stir in the fresh basil. Use an immersion blender to blend until smooth.
